Privacy Policy

Welcome to Car Wash Time!

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

We use your information to provide and improve our services, process transactions, communicate with you, and personalize your experience. We may also use your information for marketing purposes, such as sending you promotional emails or newsletters.

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide our services, comply with the law, or protect our rights. We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website. Your continued use of our services after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us
